Who we are

We are the Kozzy project, dedicated to promoting mental health and well-being. Our team is committed to providing accessible support and resources that empower individuals to take charge of their mental health.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance mental health awareness and provide accessible mental health support to all individuals. We aim to empower people to engage with their mental well-being and foster a supportive community.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where mental health support is universally accessible and prioritized, enabling individuals to lead fulfilling lives with empowered minds. We strive for a community where mental health is openly discussed and where everyone has the resources they need for a healthy mindset.

Our Services

AI-Powered Digital Companion

An AI-powered digital companion chatbot offers support, guidance, and resources for mental health, promoting well-being, understanding, and emotional resilience for users.

Communities

We provide different communities and engaging opportunities for our members to connect, share experiences, and support one another in meaningful ways.

Self help-tools

Self-help tools empower individuals to reduce anxiety, combat depression, improve mood, and enhance mental well-being through practical tips and strategies.

Therapist

Therapists provide tailored therapies to address mental health concerns, offering personalized support and guidance through convenient booking for effective sessions.
